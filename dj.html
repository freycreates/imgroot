About

From Amsterdam to international dance floors

I started DJing in 2008 at the iconic Studio 80 in Amsterdam. Today I play everything from major festivals and club nights to wedding celebrations and brand events.

I am co-founder of BOPS and have played at venues including Paradiso, as well as events in Antwerp and Milan.

Music

Pop, disco and house with an uplifting pulse

I blend iconic hits, nostalgic anthems, and fresh tracks into sets that match and amplify the mood.

That can mean relaxed selections during cocktail hour or all-out dancing deep into the night. For personal events, I can build the music around must-play songs and weave them into a set that keeps different generations moving.
